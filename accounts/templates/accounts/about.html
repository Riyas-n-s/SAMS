About SAMS

SAMS Smart Academic Management System is a modern academic platform designed to make learning and teaching more efficient and connected. Our mission is to provide students and teachers with a seamless, intuitive platform to access academic resources, track performance, and manage tasks in real-time.

With features like attendance tracking, assignment submissions, CGPA calculators, study material sharing, and No Dues management, SAMS ensures that students and teachers stay informed and organized.

Our Mission

To empower students and educators with a smart, user-friendly system that simplifies academic management and enhances learning outcomes.

Our Vision

To create a connected academic ecosystem where technology bridges the gap between students and teachers, making learning more accessible, transparent, and efficient.

Our Values

  • Innovation: Keep improving.
  • Efficiency: Simplify tasks.
  • Transparency: Clear and real-time.

How SAMS Works

A simple 4-step process to manage academics smarter and faster.

1. Login Securely

Students and teachers sign in with their credentials.

2. Access Resources

Find study materials, assignments, and notes easily.

3. Submit & Track

Submit assignments, track attendance and dues in real-time.

4. Monitor Progress

View CGPA updates and performance insights instantly.
