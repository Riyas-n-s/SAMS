Privacy Policy

At SAMS, your privacy is a top priority. This policy outlines what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user.

1. Information Collection

We collect information necessary to provide our services, including name, email, phone number, academic details, and account activity. This helps us manage attendance, assignments, and user access efficiently.

2. Use of Information

Your information is used solely for platform functionality: communication, attendance tracking, assignment submission, academic reporting, and notifications.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect data. Users are encouraged to maintain the confidentiality of their login credentials. Despite best practices, no system is 100% secure.

4. Sharing & Disclosure

Personal data is never sold. Data may only be shared with third parties for legal obligations or essential platform operations, such as notifications or integrations with educational tools.

5. User Rights

Users can request access, corrections, or deletion of personal data. Contact us at haisams@gmail.com for such requests.

6. Cookies & Analytics

We use cookies and analytics to monitor platform usage and improve user experience. No personally identifiable information is shared through analytics.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of SAMS indicates acceptance of changes.
